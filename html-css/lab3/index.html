What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. AI has the potential to revolutionize industries by automating tasks, improving efficiency, and enabling more advanced problem-solving capabilities.

In recent years, AI has made significant strides in areas such as healthcare, finance, and transportation, making processes faster and more accurate.
